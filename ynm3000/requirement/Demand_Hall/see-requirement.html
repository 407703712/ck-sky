Design of Shenzhen Hezhengrong Sales OfficeDesign of Shenzhen Hezhengrong Sales Office

CHINA GUANGDONG SHENZHEN

Published on 2018/3/6

View 281

Closed
Closed date:2018-06-05
Requirement No.:：DESIGN1284689
Requirment Details

Required Design Service Type:Engineering project

Required Design Type:Interior Design

Requirement Description

At least 30 fixed parking spaces

In the era of Vanke’s transition to a better life scenario, the sales office should be able to escape from the barriers of traditional utilitarianism, single functions, and poor experience. In combination with multimedia, technology interaction, and art installations, the sales office should be created as a “scene creation, emotional resonance”. "The city living room, to achieve the flow of each business to get through, the customer interacted with the field several times. Based on the user experience, it integra of the place with the life of the city's supporting service providers, strengthens the brand's value and influence, and creates a sales office experience field in a variety of ways including interaction, technology, and multimedia. ""

Deliverable Requirement

1. The delivery result requires a complete set of plans, renderings, reports, PPT, models, etc.;

2. The application partner needs to submit the contact telephone number, contact email address, application reason, design cost and quotation description.
